Synopsis

"Because the Internet" is Chidish Gambino's second studio album. Released in 2013, the album was nominated for "Best Rap Album" at the 2015 Grammy Awards and then best album of 2013 by Complex. Debuing and number 7 on the Billboard 200 chart, with 96,000 copies sold nation wide in the first week. This is a great album, and this website will take you through each stage of it's greatness. Enjoy!

"Clapping for the Wrong Reasons"

"Clapping for the Wrong Reasons" is a 25 minute short film that serves as a prequel to the album. Childish Gambino continues his role as "the boy" from his previous album, "Camp". He is now older and creating music in his father's mansion. The film features cameos from Gambino's team "Royalty", Chance the Rapper, Trinidad James, and adult film star Abela Anderson who plays a mysterious woman that only "the boy" can see.
